<summary>Flashing a New U-Boot on LicheePi 4A</summary>

Fedora on the LicheePi 4A requires a special U-Boot. Follow the steps below to download and flash the required U-Boot image.

-----

# 0x01 Download the U-Boot Image

Download the special U-Boot binary required for booting Fedora on the LicheePi 4A:

```
wget https://mirror.iscas.ac.cn/fedora-riscv/dl/Sipeed/LicheePi4A/fw/u-boot-with-spl.bin
```

-----

# 0x02 Flash U-Boot

Flash the downloaded U-Boot image by following the official Sipeed flashing guide below:

[Official LicheePi 4A Flashing Guide](https://wiki.sipeed.com/hardware/zh/lichee/th1520/lpi4a/4_burn_image.html#%E8%BF%9B%E5%85%A5%E7%83%A7%E5%BD%95%E6%A8%A1%E5%BC%8F)
